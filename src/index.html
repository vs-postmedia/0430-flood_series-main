Few flood plans are complete

Flood plan status for select B.C. communities.

  • A Postmedia investigation of 71 B.C. communities at risk of flooding found that less than a third had detailed, fully costed plans to manage flooding. The total cost for communities with comprehensive or partial flood mitigation plans is nearly $7.4 billion. But the price tag is certainly much higher because so few communities have fully priced plans. Scroll for more...

  • Abbotsford

    Recent estimates put the cost of addressing flood risks at $2.8 billion.

  • Richmond

    A flood protection utility fee is expected to raise $30 million per year by 2030.

  • Kelowna

    Major flooding in 2017-2018 led to work projected at about $10.7 million.

  • Williams Lake

    Severe flooding in 2020 damaged 17 bridges. Flood planning currently underway.

  • Prince George

    A 2009 flood plan worth $35.4 million did not go ahead.
